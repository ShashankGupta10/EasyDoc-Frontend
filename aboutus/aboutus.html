About Us

EasyDoc believes that answer to the world’s most critical questions lie between disciplines. Accordingly, OHT researchers employ a range of expertise—economics, epidemiology, disease modeling, risk analysis, clinical and veterinary medicine, geographic information systems, and statistics—to conduct actionable, policy-oriented research. We are experienced in addressing country-specific and regional issues as well as global challenges. Our research is renowned for innovative approaches to design and analysis, and we communicate our work to diverse stakeholders.

About Us

EasyDoc is on a mission to make quality healthcare affordable and accessible for over a billion+ Indians. We believe in empowering our users with the most accurate, comprehensive, and curated information and care, enabling them to make better healthcare decisions.
